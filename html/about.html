CosmicCatnap is an art collective in service to work that rejects modernity


We believe progress is not an inherent virtue and denounce innovation for its own sake absent of the consideration for human suffering

We believe the role of government is to ensure security, prosperity and opportunity for all by providing the means to create and live unencumbered by the demands of economic, political, or institutional systems of control.

We believe in the free exchange and preservation of art, culture, and science as only distributed knowledge can survive fate, misfortune, nature, and time allowing us to think beyond ourselves and become people we can admire

We believe math, science, and history are powerful tools which tell us global civilization is built from exploitation and avarice, and as such is doomed to fail making it our responsibility to live humble, thoughtful, meaningful lives as the last generations to experience our age
